What are bookmarklets?

Bookmarklets are bookmarks that hold javascript code, and when clicked they run the javascript. They can change, edit, destroy, and do much more to you page once clicked! It's magic! Or javascript...


Why bookmarklets?

Bookmarklets are unblocked until deliberately found out - and they're so old and out dated that barely any admins know about it. They are the stealthy ninja tasks on you bookmark bar. Plus, even if you don't have any restricting admins, they make for some pretty dope bragging rights. ;)



[IMPORTANT]

How do I add them to my bookmark bar?

To add them to you bookmarkbar, follow 6 simple steps:

1. Copy the given code. (triple-click the code and press ctrl+c)

2. Click the star on the right of your search bar, or press ctrl+d

3. Click on the "MORE" button

4. Paste the code into the "URL" box

5. Place it in the desired bookmark folder/location, and click save

6. Click on the bookmark and watch the magic happen!
